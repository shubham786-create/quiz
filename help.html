Quiz Help Desk

How to play ?

There are five subjects including Maths , English , General Knowledge , Science and Entertainment , you can play a quiz on.
Click on the "Start Quiz" button.
There are ten questions for each subjects.Each questions must be answered within 60 seconds.
Every question gives you one point.Each has four options.
If the correct option has been clicked it becomes "green", on incorrect it gets "red".

Quiz Rules :

Player will be provided only single attempt.
No question will be skipped or revisited. player will have to restart the quiz if he/she wants to replay.
Quiz can not be paused . All the questions are determined to be solved within one minute.

Login Requirements:

Players need to sign in to take the quiz.
All their "highest score" in each quiz will be saved to their profile.

Device Compatibility:

It works approximately on every browser.
Mobile and desktop support.

Technical Support:

If the quiz does not load , try refreshing the page.
Make sure JavaScript is enabled.
